Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when using the Mdabul extension.

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: The Mdabul extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information, browsing history, or keystrokes to external servers.

2. Local Storage

Any configuration settings, data preferences, or temporary files generated by the extension are stored entirely locally on your device's browser sandbox storage. This data is never synchronized with us or third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension does not share data with third-party analytical tools, tracking scripts, or ad networks.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be pushed to this page.

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out via our repository link.
